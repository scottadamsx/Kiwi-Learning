Minesweeper

Reveal every safe square on the 9×9 board without hitting a mine — you get one life.

How to play
  1. Click a square to reveal it. A number tells you how many mines touch that square.
  2. Use the numbers to deduce which neighbours are safe and which hide mines.
  3. Flag the squares you believe are mines so you don't click them by accident.
  4. Clear every non-mine square to win. Clicking a mine ends the round.
Controls

Click to reveal · right-click (or long-press) to flag

How you earn

Clear the whole board to claim. There are no retries — one mine ends it.

Tip

A "1" touching exactly one hidden square means that square is the mine — flag it and the rest become safe.
