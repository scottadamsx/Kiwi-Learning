Fruit Match

Score 300 points by lining up matching fruit.

How to play
  1. Swap two neighbouring fruit to make a row or column of 3 or more of the same kind.
  2. Matched fruit clear, everything above falls, and new fruit drop in — sometimes chaining more matches.
  3. Only swaps that create a match are allowed.
  4. Keep matching until you hit the 300-point target.
Controls

Click a fruit, then click an adjacent one to swap

How you earn

Reaching 300 points unlocks the claim.

Tip

Matches of 4+ and chain reactions are worth far more — look for setups before making the obvious 3.
