Odd One Out

Spot the one tile that's a slightly different shade.

How to play
  1. A grid of tiles appears, all the same colour except one.
  2. Click the odd tile out.
  3. Each level the difference gets subtler and the grid grows.
  4. Clear as many levels as you can.
Controls

Click the tile that looks different

How you earn

The bonus scales with levels cleared.

Tip

Scan the corners and edges first — the odd tile is easiest to catch against fewer neighbours.
