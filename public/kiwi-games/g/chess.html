Chess

Checkmate the CPU's king — trap it so it can't escape capture.

How to play
  1. You play White and move first; tap one of your pieces to select it.
  2. Its legal destination squares light up with dots — tap one to move there.
  3. Capture the CPU's pieces by moving onto them; pawns reaching the back rank become queens.
  4. Put the CPU's king in check, then close off every escape to deliver checkmate.
Controls

Tap a piece, then tap a highlighted square

How you earn

Your first capture unlocks the claim; checkmating the CPU pays the top bonus.

Tip

Develop your knights and bishops early and castle your king to safety before attacking.
