Whack-a-Mole

Whack 12 moles before the 30-second timer runs out.

How to play
  1. Moles pop out of the holes at random.
  2. Click a mole while it's up to score a hit.
  3. They only stay up briefly, so be quick.
  4. Land 12 hits before time expires.
Controls

Click the moles as they appear

How you earn

12 hits inside the timer unlocks the claim.

Tip

Rest your cursor in the middle of the grid so you're never far from the next mole.
