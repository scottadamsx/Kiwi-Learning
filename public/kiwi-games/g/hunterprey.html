Kiwi Hunt

Two players on one device — outscore each other before the round timer runs out.

How to play
  1. Player 1 is the prey: use the arrow keys to dodge and grab the targets that spawn (+1 each).
  2. Player 2 is the hunter: aim the crosshair with the mouse and click to shoot the prey (+1 each hit).
  3. A hit briefly stuns and respawns the prey.
  4. When the timer ends, the higher score wins — then swap roles and rematch.
Controls

P1: arrow keys · P2: mouse + click (best on a laptop/desktop)

How you earn

Finish a full round to unlock the claim.

Tip

As the prey, keep moving in arcs; as the hunter, lead your shots ahead of where the kiwi is running.
