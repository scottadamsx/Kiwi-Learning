Kiwi Unicorns

Be the first to gather a stable of 6 unicorns, racing the CPU.

How to play
  1. Each turn draw a card, then take one action: play a card or draw an extra.
  2. Play Unicorn cards into your stable to grow it toward 6.
  3. Use Magic and Action cards to help yourself or disrupt the CPU's stable.
  4. Hold a cancel card to neigh an opponent's big play out of turn.
Controls

Tap a card in your hand to play it

How you earn

Reach 3 unicorns to unlock the claim; winning the game pays the top bonus.

Tip

Save a cancel card for the CPU's steal or destroy plays — timing it well swings the whole game.
